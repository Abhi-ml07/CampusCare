About CampusCare

CampusCare is a student-centric platform designed to empower students to report and resolve campus issues efficiently. Our mission is to create a more responsive and inclusive campus environment by connecting students with the right resources and authorities.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, CampusCare was born out of the frustration of students facing unresolved issues on campus. We wanted to create a solution that would give students a voice and make it easier for them to get help when they need it.

What We Offer

  • Issue Reporting: Students can easily report issues related to academics, facilities, administration, and more.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Users can track the status of their reported issues in real-time and receive updates.
  • Community Engagement: Students can like and comment on issues, fostering a sense of community and support.
  • Resource Hub: Access guides, FAQs, and contact information for various campus services.

Our Team

CampusCare is developed and maintained by a passionate team of students who are committed to improving campus life. We are always looking for feedback and new ideas to make our platform better.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or want to get involved, please don't hesitate to reach out through our contact page.
